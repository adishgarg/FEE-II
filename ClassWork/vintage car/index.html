ABOUT US
___

We are a team of passionate car enthusiasts who specialize in vintage cars. We have been in the business for over 20 years and have a reputation for quality work and excellent customer service. Our team of experts can handle everything from general repairs to custom paintwork and upholstery. We take pride in our work and treat every car as if it were our own. If you have a vintage car that needs some love and attention, we are the team for you.
